Filter Strategy:
Category Filter (Single): Match events where the selected category is EITHER:
The main_category_id in jos_eb_events, OR
Any category in jos_eb_event_categories (main OR additional)
Additional Categories (Multiple): Events MUST have ALL selected additional categories
Location Filter: Match location_id
Date Filter: Match event_date
